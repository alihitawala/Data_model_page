Data Science

Buying and selling of used bikes is a huge industry. For this project, we crawl data from 2 of the most popular Indian websites that let you buy and sell used bikes - BikeDekho and BikeWale. Our goal is to extract relevant information from the both the websites and match them to find if the exact same bike is being sold at a cheaper price on the other website. We have crawled 4953 BikeDekho postings and about 25412 BikeWale listings.
